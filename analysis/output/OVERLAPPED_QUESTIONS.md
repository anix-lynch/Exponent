## üéì HOW TO MASTER THESE OVERLAPPED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

### Step 1: Prepare STAR Stories (2-3 hours)

For **Behavioral** questions, prepare 10-15 STAR stories covering:
- Leadership & influence
- Problem solving & crisis management
- Stakeholder management & communication
- Data-driven decision making
- Strategic thinking & planning
- Failure & learning
- Team collaboration

### Step 2: Practice SQL (1-2 hours)

Focus on these SQL topics:
- JOINs (INNER, LEFT, RIGHT, FULL)
- Aggregations (GROUP BY, HAVING)
- Window functions (ROW_NUMBER, RANK, LAG, LEAD)
- Subqueries & CTEs
- Query optimization

### Step 3: Review Frameworks (1 hour)

Memorize these frameworks:
- **Analytical:** MECE, 5 Whys, Root Cause Analysis
- **Strategy:** Porter's 5 Forces, SWOT, Business Model Canvas
- **Problem Solving:** Define ‚Üí Analyze ‚Üí Options ‚Üí Decide ‚Üí Execute
- **Stakeholder:** Identify ‚Üí Prioritize ‚Üí Engage ‚Üí Align ‚Üí Monitor

### Step 4: Practice Adapting (ongoing)

- Use the same STAR story for multiple questions
- Adjust emphasis based on what they're testing
- Always tie back to business impact
- Quantify results when possible
