## All 13 Questions:

1. What's your favorite product and why?
2. You're the PM for one of your favorite products. What is your strategy for the next year?
3. How would you improve your favorite product?
4. Give me three product enhancements for your favorite product.
5. Describe how you would 10X your favorite product.
6. Name your top three favorite products, select one, and outline a plan to scale it by tenfold.
7. Tell me about your favorite product.
8. What are the company's pain points of your favorite product?
9. How would you measure the success of your favorite product as the PMO?
10. How do we increase revenue (based on favorite product)?
11. You're the CEO or VP of your favorite product. How would you structure your organization and why?
12. Tell me about your favorite product. How would you build its competitor?
13. List your top three favorite products. How will you measure success and mention all possible areas?
