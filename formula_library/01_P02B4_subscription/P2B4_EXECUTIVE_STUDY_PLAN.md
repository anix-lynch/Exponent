### Why Subscription Metrics Matter

**Key Insight:** Subscription products are about **retention and lifetime value**. Metrics must capture both acquisition (getting subscribers) and retention (keeping them) to maximize LTV.

**Business Model Characteristics:**
- Recurring revenue: predictable, scalable
- Churn is critical: losing subscribers hurts more than gaining new ones
- Engagement drives retention: active users stay longer
- LTV/CAC ratio: must be > 3:1 to be profitable
