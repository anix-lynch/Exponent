## üìä CONCEPT BUCKET BREAKDOWN

### BUCKET 1: Execution & Ownership Framework
**Questions:** ~20 | **Priority:** üü¢ GREEN (Master this)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **What:** "Tell me about a time you missed a deadline" or "Project that went off track" - execution and ownership questions
- **Framework:** Situation ‚Üí Task ‚Üí Action ‚Üí Result (STAR)
- **Situation:** Context (What was the situation? When? Where?), Challenge (Deadline pressure, Resource constraints, Project failure, Unexpected challenges), What made it difficult?
- **Task:** Your responsibility (What was I accountable for?), Goal (What needed to be achieved?), Constraints (Time, resources, dependencies)
- **Action:** How I handled pressure (Prioritization, Communication, Problem-solving), How I recovered from failure (Ownership, Root cause analysis, Recovery plan), How I took ownership (Taking responsibility, Not blaming others, Leading the fix), How I delivered under constraints (Scope cuts, Resource reallocation, Timeline adjustments, Tradeoffs), Why I did it this way (My reasoning, Tradeoffs considered)
- **Result:** Outcome (What happened? What was the result? Did I achieve the goal?), Impact (What was the impact?), Learning (What did I learn? What would I do differently?)

**Concrete Examples:**
- "Missed deadline: Situation (Project X, deadline pressure, constraints), Task (My role, goal), Action (How I handled it, ownership, recovery), Result (Outcome, impact, learning)"
- "Project failure: Situation (Project X, failure, context), Task (My role, goal), Action (How I handled failure, ownership, recovery), Result (Outcome, learning, improvements)"

**Representative Questions (Do 5 only):**
- Q1971: Tell me about a time when you missed a deadline. (deadline miss angle)
- Q222: Describe a time when your project failed. (project failure angle)
- Q1625: If you join a project that is 25% complete and has used 85% of resources, what would be your next steps? (project recovery angle)
- Q1004: Have you ever faced a project deadline that was earlier than expected? How did you handle it and what was the outcome? (deadline pressure angle)
- Q1995: Tell me about a time when you took ownership of a project you weren't originally responsible for. (ownership angle)
