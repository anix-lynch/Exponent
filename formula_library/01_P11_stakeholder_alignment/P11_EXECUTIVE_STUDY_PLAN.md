**Questions:** ~20 | **Priority:** ðŸŸ¢ GREEN (Master this)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **What:** "How do you influence without authority?" or "Get buy-in without power" - core alignment framework
- **Framework:** Understand Incentives â†’ Address Concerns â†’ Build Coalition â†’ Decide
- **Understand Incentives:** Identify stakeholders (Exec, Product, Engineering, Legal/Ops/Sales), For each: Goal (what success looks like), Fear (what they're protecting against), Power (can block/influence/approve)

- **Decide:** Restate shared goal, Present aligned proposal, Call decision explicitly, Document + communicate outcome

**Concrete Examples:**
- "Influence without authority: Understand what each stakeholder cares about (Product: UX, Engineering: quality, Exec: growth), Address their concerns (guardrails, data, mitigations), Build coalition (align with influential stakeholders first), Decide (present aligned proposal)"
