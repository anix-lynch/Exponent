**Even when "behavioral" isn't mentioned, look for these keywords/phrases:**

### Explicit Behavioral Keywords:

### Implicit P15 Indicators:
- **Past experience questions:** "Tell me about a time when...", "Describe a situation where...", "Give me an example of..."

- **STAR format:** Questions asking for Situation, Task, Action, Result
- **Soft skills:** Leadership, collaboration, conflict, decision-making, problem-solving

- **P15 (Behavioral):** "Tell me about a time when..." â†’ Focus: Past experience, STAR format

- **P15 (Behavioral):** "Tell me about a time when you influenced stakeholders" â†’ Focus: Past experience, STAR
- **P11 (Stakeholder Alignment):** "How do you align stakeholders?" â†’ Focus: Framework, process

### P15 vs P12 Distinction:
- **P15 (Behavioral):** "Tell me about a time when you fixed a broken process" â†’ Focus: Past experience, STAR
