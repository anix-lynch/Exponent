## üéØ EXECUTIVE SCOPE (10-15 min)

### Why E-commerce/Fintech Metrics Matter

**Key Insight:** E-commerce/Fintech products are about **converting visitors to transactions**. Metrics must capture both conversion efficiency (funnel) and transaction value (AOV, repeat purchases) to maximize GMV and revenue.

**Business Model Characteristics:**
- Transaction-based: revenue from successful purchases/payments
- Funnel-driven: conversion rate is critical
- Trust-dependent: payment success and security matter
- Repeat purchases: LTV from repeat customers

### BUCKET 2: Conversion Funnel Metrics
**Questions:** ~1 | **Priority:** üü¢ GREEN (High-yield)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **Funnel Metrics:** Conversion rate, cart abandonment, checkout completion, add-to-cart rate
- **Key Insight:** Each funnel step has drop-off - measure and optimize each step
- **Measurement:** Conversion rate by step, abandonment rate, completion rate
- **Optimization:** Identify biggest drop-off, hypothesize causes, test fixes

**Concrete Examples:**
- "Conversion rate: purchases / visitors - measures funnel efficiency"
- "Cart abandonment: % of carts not completed - measures friction"
- "Checkout completion: % of checkouts completed - measures payment success"

**Representative Question Types (Do 5 only):**
