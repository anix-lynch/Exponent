## ðŸ“Œ Sample: North Star Metric Question

**Question:**
"What is the north star metric for [Product]?"

**Answer Framework:**

1. **Define NSM**: What is THE single metric that best represents user + business value? What do users care about? What drives business? How do they align? Why this metric? Is it actionable, measurable, and predictive?

2. **Input KPIs**: What drives the NSM? User behavior drivers, supply/quality drivers, monetization drivers. What are the controllable levers? What can we change to move the NSM?

3. **Leading Indicators**: What are the early signals? Short-term behaviors, funnel entry/activation signals, quality proxies. Why are they leading? Faster feedback, predict NSM movement.

4. **Guardrails**: What must NOT break? User trust, long-term health, cost/abuse/compliance. What are the safety metrics? Prevent gaming, protect against unintended consequences.

## ðŸŽ¯ Key Principles

- **Single metric focus**: One NSM, not 10 metrics
- **User + Business alignment**: Must represent both
- **Actionable**: Can we influence it?
- **Predictive**: Does it predict long-term success?
- **Guardrails**: Always include safety metrics
