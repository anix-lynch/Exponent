## üîç HOW TO IDENTIFY P15A (PERSONAL & MOTIVATION) QUESTIONS

**Even when "personal" or "motivation" isn't mentioned, look for these keywords/phrases:**

### Explicit Personal Keywords:
- "tell me about yourself", "why did you become", "what are you passionate about"
- "what motivates you", "what are you looking for", "why this company/role"
- "career choice", "values", "culture fit", "excited about"

### Implicit P15A Indicators:
- **Personal questions:** "Tell me about yourself", "Why did you become a PM/Engineer?", "What are you passionate about?"
- **Motivation questions:** "What motivates you?", "What are you looking for in your next role?", "Why this company?"
- **Values questions:** "Which company values resonate with you?", "What matters to you?"

### P15A vs P16 Distinction:
- **P15A (Personal & Motivation):** "Tell me about yourself" ‚Üí Focus: Who you are, what motivates you
- **P16 (Intent/Motivation):** "Why this company/role?" ‚Üí Focus: Why this specific opportunity

### Red Flags (NOT P15A):
- "Tell me about a time when..." ‚Üí P15B-P15F (Other behavioral categories)
- "Why this company?" ‚Üí P16 (Intent/Motivation)
