### Implicit P15E Indicators:
- **Leadership questions:** "Tell me about influencing stakeholders", "How do you work with execs?", "Time you led a project"
- **Collaboration questions:** "Tell me about cross-functional work", "How do you handle disagreement?", "Time you worked with different teams"
- **Influence questions:** "How do you influence without authority?", "Tell me about influencing a roadmap", "How do you build trust?"

### P15E vs P11 Distinction:
- **P15E (Leadership & Collaboration):** "Tell me about a time you influenced stakeholders" ‚Üí Focus: Past experience, STAR format
- **P11 (Stakeholder Alignment):** "How do you align stakeholders?" ‚Üí Focus: Framework, process

### P15E vs P10 Distinction:
- **P15E (Leadership & Collaboration):** "Tell me about working with execs" ‚Üí Focus: Past experience, STAR
- **P10 (Executive Communication):** "How would you explain X to executives?" ‚Üí Focus: Communication framework

### Red Flags (NOT P15E):
- "How do you align stakeholders?" ‚Üí P11 (Stakeholder Alignment) - if framework-focused
- "How would you explain X to executives?" ‚Üí P10 (Executive Communication) - if framework-focused

## üìä CONCEPT BUCKET BREAKDOWN

### BUCKET 1: Leadership & Collaboration Framework
**Questions:** ~50 | **Priority:** üü¢ GREEN (Master this)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **What:** "Tell me about influencing stakeholders" or "Time you worked cross-functionally" - leadership and collaboration questions
- **Framework:** Situation ‚Üí Task ‚Üí Action (Leadership/Collaboration Focus) ‚Üí Result (STAR)
- **Situation:** Context (What was the situation? When? Where?), Challenge (Cross-functional work, Conflict or disagreement, Influencing without authority, Communication challenge), What made it difficult?
- **Task:** Your responsibility (What was my responsibility?), Goal (What needed to be achieved? What was the goal?)
- **Action:** Communication approach (How I communicated - clear, transparent, tailored to audience), Building relationships (How I built trust - consistency, respect, value), Resolving conflict (How I resolved - understanding both sides, finding common ground, win-win solutions), Influencing stakeholders (How I influenced - data, relationships, alignment, framing), Why I did it this way (My reasoning, Stakeholder considerations)
- **Result:** Outcome (What happened? What was the result? Did I achieve the goal?), Impact (What was the impact?), Learning (What did I learn? Focus on relationship building and influence, not just outcome)

**Concrete Examples:**
- "Influence stakeholders: Situation (Stakeholders, disagreement, context), Task (My role, goal), Action (How I influenced - communication, relationships, data, alignment), Result (Outcome, impact, learning)"
- "Cross-functional work: Situation (Cross-functional team, challenge, context), Task (My role, goal), Action (How I collaborated - communication, alignment, influence), Result (Outcome, impact, learning)"
