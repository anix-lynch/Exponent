## üîç HOW TO IDENTIFY P16 (INTENT / MOTIVATION) QUESTIONS

**Even when "intent" or "motivation" isn't mentioned, look for these keywords/phrases:**

### Explicit Intent Keywords:
- "why do you want to work at", "why are you interested in", "what excites you about"
- "why this company", "why this role", "why now", "what attracts you"
- "mission", "values", "resonates", "motivation", "interest"

### Implicit P16 Indicators:
- **Company questions:** "Why do you want to work at X?", "Why are you interested in X company?"
- **Role questions:** "What excites you about this role?", "Why are you interested in this role?"
- **Mission questions:** "What part of our mission resonates with you?", "Which company values resonate?"
- **Timing questions:** "Why now?", "Why is this the right time?", "Why are you looking?"

### P16 vs P15A Distinction:
- **P16 (Intent/Motivation):** "Why do you want to work at X?" ‚Üí Focus: Company, role, mission, personal fit, why now
- **P15A (Personal & Motivation):** "Why did you become a PM?" ‚Üí Focus: Personal story, career choice, values

### P16 vs P15 Distinction:
- **P16 (Intent/Motivation):** "Why do you want to work at X?" ‚Üí Focus: Future intent, company/role fit
- **P15 (Behavioral):** "Tell me about a time when..." ‚Üí Focus: Past experience, STAR format

### Red Flags (NOT P16):
- "Tell me about a time when..." ‚Üí P15 (Behavioral)
- "Why did you become a PM?" ‚Üí P15A (Personal & Motivation)
- "How would you design X?" ‚Üí P13 (Product Design)

## üìä CONCEPT BUCKET BREAKDOWN

### BUCKET 1: Company & Role Fit
**Questions:** ~30 | **Priority:** üü¢ GREEN (Master this)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **What:** "Why do you want to work at X?" or "What excites you about this role?" - core intent framework
- **Framework:** Company ‚Üí Role ‚Üí Mission ‚Üí Personal Fit ‚Üí Why Now
- **Company:** What attracts you to this company? (Products/services - What do they build?, Market position - Industry leader, startup, scale-up?, Culture - What's their reputation?, Growth trajectory - Where are they heading?), Research (Company values/mission, Recent news/initiatives, Leadership/team)
- **Role:** What excites you about this role? (Responsibilities - What will you do?, Impact - What problems will you solve?, Learning - What will you learn?, Growth - How will you develop?), Fit (Skills match - Your strengths align, Experience - Your background fits, Interest - You're passionate about this)
- **Mission:** What part of our mission resonates with you? (Company mission - What do they stand for?, Your values - What matters to you?, Alignment - How do they connect?), Impact (User impact - Who benefits?, Industry impact - How does this change things?, Personal impact - Why is this meaningful to you?)
- **Personal Fit:** Why you (Skills - What can you contribute?, Experience - What have you done?, Passion - What drives you?), Why this role (Career stage - Where are you?, Goals - What do you want to achieve?, Timing - Why is this the right time?)
- **Why Now:** Timing (Career stage - Ready for this challenge, Market timing - Industry trends align, Personal timing - Life circumstances), Commitment (Research - You've done your homework, Enthusiasm - You're genuinely excited, Long-term - You see a future here)

**Concrete Examples:**
- "Why work at Google: Company (Products: Search, Cloud, AI, Market: Industry leader, Culture: Innovation, Growth: Expanding), Role (Responsibilities: PM role, Impact: Scale products, Learning: Technical depth, Growth: Career growth), Mission (Mission: Organize information, Values: Innovation, Impact: Global impact), Personal Fit (Skills: Product, Experience: X years, Passion: Technology), Why Now (Career: Ready for challenge, Market: AI growth, Commitment: Excited, long-term)"
- "Why this role: Company (Research done, values align), Role (Exciting responsibilities, impact, learning), Mission (Resonates, meaningful), Personal Fit (Skills match, experience fits, passionate), Why Now (Right time, committed)"

**1Ô∏è‚É£ Company** ‚Üí What attracts me to this company?

**2Ô∏è‚É£ Role** ‚Üí What excites me about this role?

  - **Impact:** What impact will I have? Who benefits?
