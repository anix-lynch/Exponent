## ðŸ“Œ Sample: Intent Question

**Question:**
"Why do you want to work at [Company]?"

**Answer Framework:**

1. **Company**: What attracts you to this company? Research their products/services, market position, culture, growth trajectory. What did you learn about their values, recent news, leadership?

2. **Role**: What excites you about this role? What responsibilities, impact, learning opportunities, and growth potential? Why are you a good fit? How do your skills, experience, and interests align?

3. **Mission**: What part of their mission resonates with you? How does the company mission align with your values? Why does this matter? What's the user impact, industry impact, and personal meaning?

4. **Personal Fit**: Why you? What makes you unique? What skills, experience, and passion can you contribute? Why this role? Why now? What's your career stage, goals, and timing?

5. **Why Now**: Why is this the right moment? Career stage, market timing, personal timing. Why are you serious? Show you've done research, you're genuinely excited, and you see a long-term future here.

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Key Principles

- **Be specific**: Research the company, don't give generic answers
- **Show enthusiasm**: Genuine excitement is contagious
- **Connect to yourself**: Link company/role to your values and goals
- **Be honest**: Authentic answers resonate more than scripted ones
- **Show commitment**: Demonstrate you've thought about this seriously
