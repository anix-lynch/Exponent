### Why Advertising Metrics Matter

**Key Insight:** Advertising products are about **balancing advertiser value (ROAS) and user experience**. Metrics must capture both advertiser outcomes (clicks, conversions) and platform health (user experience, ad quality).

**Business Model Characteristics:**
- Two-sided: advertisers (demand) + users (supply)
- Performance-based: advertisers pay for results (clicks, conversions)
- User experience: must balance ad load with revenue
- Quality matters: bad ads hurt both advertisers and users

### BUCKET 2: Performance Metrics (CTR, CPC, ROAS)
**Questions:** ~2 | **Priority:** üü¢ GREEN (High-yield)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **CTR:** Click-Through Rate = clicks / impressions - measures ad relevance
- **CPC:** Cost Per Click - advertiser cost per click
- **ROAS:** Return on Ad Spend - revenue / ad spend, target > 3:1
- **Key Insight:** Higher CTR = better relevance, lower CPC = better efficiency, higher ROAS = better outcomes
- **Measurement:** CTR by ad format, CPC by targeting, ROAS by campaign

**Concrete Examples:**
- "CTR: clicks / impressions - measures ad relevance, target 1-3%"
- "CPC: cost per click - advertiser cost, lower is better"
- "ROAS: revenue / ad spend - advertiser ROI, target > 3:1"

**Representative Question Types (Do 5 only):**
- How do you measure CTR?
- What is ROAS and why does it matter?
- How would you improve ad performance?
- What's the relationship between CTR and CPC?
- How do you measure conversion rate?

**1Ô∏è‚É£ CTR** ‚Üí Click-Through Rate = clicks/impressions (target 1-3%).

**2Ô∏è‚É£ CPC** ‚Üí Cost Per Click (lower is better).

**3Ô∏è‚É£ ROAS** ‚Üí Return on Ad Spend = revenue/ad spend (target > 3:1).

**4Ô∏è‚É£ Input KPIs** ‚Üí Ad relevance score, targeting accuracy, ad format.

**5Ô∏è‚É£ Guardrails** ‚Üí User experience (must not degrade), ad quality (must be relevant), advertiser satisfaction (must be positive).
