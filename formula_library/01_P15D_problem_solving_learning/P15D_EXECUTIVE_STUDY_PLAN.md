## üìä CONCEPT BUCKET BREAKDOWN

### BUCKET 1: Problem Solving & Learning Framework
**Questions:** ~30 | **Priority:** üü¢ GREEN (Master this)

**Board Slide Bullets:**
- **What:** "Tell me about a complex problem you solved" or "Time you learned something quickly" - problem-solving questions
- **Framework:** Situation ‚Üí Task ‚Üí Action (Problem-Solving Focus) ‚Üí Result (STAR)
- **Situation:** Context (What was the problem? When? Where?), Challenge (What was the complexity? Complex technical problem, Ambiguous situation, Broken process, New technology to learn), What made it difficult?
- **Task:** Your responsibility (What was my responsibility?), Goal (What needed to be solved/learned? What was the goal?)
- **Action:** How I broke down the problem (Breaking into smaller parts, Identifying root cause, Prioritizing), Research and learning (How I learned - research, documentation, experts, practice), Experimentation (How I tested - prototypes, experiments, iterations), Iteration (How I refined - feedback, improvements, iterations), Why I did it this way (My reasoning, Alternatives considered)
- **Result:** Outcome (What happened? What was the result? Did I solve it?), Impact (What was the impact?), Learning (What did I learn? Focus on process and learning, not just outcome)

**Concrete Examples:**
- "Complex problem: Situation (Problem X, complexity, context), Task (My role, goal), Action (How I approached - breakdown, research, experimentation, iteration), Result (Outcome, impact, learning)"
- "Quick learning: Situation (New technology/task, urgency, context), Task (My role, goal), Action (How I learned - research, practice, iteration), Result (Outcome, application, learning)"

**Representative Questions (Do 5 only):**
- Q1987: Tell me about a time when you solved a complex problem and how you went about it. (complex problem angle)
- Q2060: Tell me about a time you had to learn something quickly. (quick learning angle)
- Q902: Give me an example of a time when you had to work outside your comfort zone. How did you gain expertise in that area? (comfort zone angle)
- Q1922: Tell me about a time when you gave a simple solution to a complex problem. (simple solution angle)
- Q2069: Tell me about a time you improved a broken process or system. (process improvement angle)
