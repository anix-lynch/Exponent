## Key Takeaways

1. **Every P3 question fits ONE bucket** - identify it first

3. **Don't mix approaches** - pick one and stick with it
4. **Funnel drop-off is the classic P3** - but not all P3 questions are funnels
5. **Strategy questions often masquerade as metrics** - recognize them
