## üí° Product Marketing Manager Role Overview

Product Marketing Managers are the bridge between product, sales, and marketing. Key responsibilities:

- **Go-To-Market Strategy**: Develop comprehensive GTM strategies for product launches
- **Product Positioning**: Craft compelling positioning that differentiates in the market
- **Product Launch**: Orchestrate complex, multi-stakeholder product launches
- **Messaging & Communication**: Create clear, compelling messaging for target audiences
- **Market Research**: Gather and synthesize market insights to inform strategy
- **Competitive Analysis**: Analyze competitors and develop winning strategies
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Work effectively with product, sales, and marketing teams
- **Sales Enablement**: Equip sales teams with tools and training to win
- **Customer Insights**: Gather, analyze, and apply customer feedback
