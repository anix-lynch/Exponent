## üí° Chief of Staff Role Overview

The Chief of Staff is the CEO's strategic partner and force multiplier. Key responsibilities:

- **Strategic Planning**: Translate CEO vision into executable plans
- **Cross-Functional Leadership**: Drive alignment across teams
- **Communication**: Bridge gaps between leadership and organization
- **Problem Solving**: Tackle the CEO's hardest challenges
- **Execution**: Turn strategy into results
