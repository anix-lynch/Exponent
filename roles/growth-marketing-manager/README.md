## ğŸ’¡ Growth Marketing Manager Role Overview

Growth Marketing Managers drive user and revenue growth through data-driven experimentation. Key responsibilities:

- **Growth Strategy**: Develop and execute strategies to scale user acquisition and revenue
- **Customer Acquisition**: Optimize channels and campaigns to efficiently acquire customers
- **Retention & Engagement**: Build loops to keep users engaged and reduce churn
- **Analytics & Metrics**: Define, track, and act on growth metrics (CAC, LTV, etc.)
- **A/B Testing**: Design and execute rigorous experiments to drive growth
- **Channel Strategy**: Identify, test, and scale effective marketing channels
- **Product-Led Growth**: Leverage the product itself as a growth driver
