## üí° Business Analyst Role Overview

Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical solutions. Key skills:

- **Data Analysis**: Business problem solving, root cause analysis, trend analysis
- **SQL**: Joins, aggregation, window functions, query optimization
- **Business Metrics & KPIs**: Defining and tracking success metrics
- **Estimation & Market Sizing**: Quantitative reasoning
- **Requirements Gathering**: Eliciting and documenting business needs
- **Stakeholder Management**: Communication with leadership and teams
- **Process Improvement**: Identifying and implementing efficiencies
- **Financial Analysis**: Revenue, cost, ROI analysis
