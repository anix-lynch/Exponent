## üí° Product Analyst Role Overview

Product Analysts use data to drive product decisions. Key skills:

- **Product Metrics**: Defining, tracking, and interpreting success metrics
- **A/B Testing**: Designing, analyzing, and interpreting experiments
- **Data Analysis**: Feature impact, user behavior, funnel analysis, retention
- **Product Strategy**: Using data to influence product decisions
- **Product Sense**: Understanding user needs and product improvements
- **Dashboard & Visualization**: Building executive dashboards
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Working with PMs and leadership
- **SQL**: Querying product data
