### Universal Principles for Finance & Strategy Interviews

1. **Start with the business objective** - What decision needs to be made?
2. **Quantify everything** - Use numbers, percentages, and financial metrics
3. **Show your thinking** - Walk through your logic step-by-step
4. **Consider trade-offs** - Every decision has pros and cons
5. **Tie back to impact** - How does this affect the bottom line?

## ğŸ’¡ Final Tips

### For All Finance & Strategy Interviews:

1. **Think like a CFO** - Focus on profitability, efficiency, and growth
2. **Use frameworks** - SWOT, Porter's 5 Forces, Unit Economics, etc.
3. **Be hypothesis-driven** - Start with a hypothesis, then validate with data
4. **Communicate clearly** - Finance people often present to non-finance stakeholders
5. **Show business acumen** - Understand how finance enables business strategy

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:

- âŒ Being too theoretical - use real examples and numbers
- âŒ Ignoring constraints - always consider budget, time, resources
- âŒ Forgetting the "so what?" - explain business impact
- âŒ Overcomplicating - simple, clear analysis is better than complex models
